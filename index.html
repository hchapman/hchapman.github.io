About Me

I am currently a mathematics graduate student at the University of Georgia working towards a Ph.D. degree. My research interests primarily lie within Topology and Geometry. I am studying under the direction of Jason Cantarella. Recently I have been studying knots in the graph-theoretic view of knot diagrams.

During the 2012-2013 school year, I was supported by the University of Georgia's VIGRE II grant. I graduated from Bowdoin College in May of 2011 with a B.A. in Mathematics and Computer Science, with Honors in Mathematics.

Teaching
